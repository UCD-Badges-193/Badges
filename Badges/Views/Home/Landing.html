A Tradition of Land-based Learning

More than 100 years ago, UC Davis started out as a farm for the University of California.

Leader in the Field

Out of that early connection to the land, we’ve grown to be the premier agricultural research institution in the world, and a model within the land-grant university system.

Sustainable Innovation

Now in our second century, we continue to innovate at the frontiers of food and agriculture, and bring a strong focus on sustainability to that work.

Next Generation of Leaders

Students from around the world are drawn to work at this interface of Food, Agriculture and Sustainability at UC Davis. Many of these students are part of an innovative undergraduate major in Sustainable Agriculture and Food Systems (SA&FS).

The Badges Experiment

Students in the SA&FS major have the unique opportunity to participate in one of the first badge systems in higher education to be integrated into the formal curriculum, as a complement to traditional grading and evaluation.

Follow along as we show you how our badge system works and how it’s transforming student learning at UC Davis.
